Security Awareness

Stay safe online by following these essential security tips.

Cybersecurity Best Practices

Use Strong Passwords

Ensure your passwords are long, unique, and use a mix of characters.

Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Add an extra layer of security by enabling 2FA on your accounts.

Beware of Phishing Attacks

Do not click on suspicious emails, links, or attachments.

Security Awareness Tips

Keep Your Software Updated

Ensure your operating system, apps, and antivirus are up to date.

Secure Your Wi-Fi

Use strong passwords and WPA2/WPA3 encryption to prevent unauthorized access.

Dispose of Data Securely

Shred documents and wipe old devices before disposal.

Be Cautious on Social Media

Limit the amount of personal information you share online.

Monitor Your Bank Transactions

Regularly check your statements for unauthorized transactions.

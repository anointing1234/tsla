Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our platform, payments, and more.

General

 How long does it take to process my withdrawal?
Bank Wires: 1–3 business days (max).
E-Wallets: Within 1 business day.
 Do I have to withdraw my funds only in Bitcoin?
You can choose to withdraw your funds as cash directly to your bank account or to an e-wallet as Bitcoin. Whichever is more suitable for you.
 How can I withdraw funds from my account?
As soon as your trading cycle is complete, you will be given the option to request a withdrawal and have your payout processed!
 Are we a financial pyramid?
No. We offer our clients strategies that have been proven over many years of offline and online work.
 How much is the minimum deposit amount?
£100 is the minimum plan amount.

Payment

 Can I register without a deposit?
Yes. You can register your account without making a deposit, but you won’t be eligible to profit from trades until you deposit.
 How can I deposit funds into my account?
All funds are deposited in Bitcoin. You will purchase the amount in Bitcoin and send it to your personal investor wallet address.
 Where can I find my investor ID/wallet address?
Immediately after signing up, your personal investor ID/wallet address is displayed on your Dashboard once you log in.
 How long does it take to process my deposit?
Typically within one hour (max).

Security & Privacy

 Does my trading manager have access to my funds?
No. They can never withdraw your account funds. Trading managers only manage trades carried out on your account.
 If I forget my password, can I reset it?
Yes. You can reset your password by clicking on "Forgot Password" on the login page. A reset link will be sent to your registered email immediately.
 Is depositing safe and secure?
Yes. Depositing money to your account is safe, private, and secure. All transactions are communicated using SSL technology, ensuring that your personal information is protected.
 Can I lose my investment with Access Forex Concierge?
All funds are traded securely and only on high-probability signals. Even in the event of unexpected market conditions, we strive to protect your initial capital.
